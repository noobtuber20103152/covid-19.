How to fight CORONA

3 Optimal way to fight CORONA

Boost Immune system

The immune system is a complex network of cells and proteins that defends the body against infection. The immune system keeps a record of every germ (microbe) it has ever defeated so it can recognise and destroy the microbe quickly if it enters the body again.

Use Senitizer

Using hand sanitizer is effective against the novel coronavirus which is an enveloped virus. Alcohol-based hand sanitizers are effective in removing these enveloped viruses by inactivating the envelope, thus making it non-infectious.
